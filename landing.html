SISTEM


A Capstone Project Created using MERN Stack

Purpose

A website designed for the Department of Computer and Information Sciences and Mathematics (DCISM) at the University of San Carlos, designed to assist student enrollment, concerns and study plan creation.

Dashboard Page

The student dashboard page of SISTEM provides a comprehensive overview of a student's progress, current study load, and any submitted requests or petitions. All this information is conveniently displayed on a single page for easy student monitoring.

Grades Submission

Displayed on the screen is the student's grades page, a backbone feature of SISTEM which parses data from a PDF extracted from the USC portal (ISMIS). It is designed to indicate whether a student has passed or failed, shown as 0 for fail and 1 for pass. Submitting grades is required for students to generate their study loads.


A "Regex" pattern is being used to extract data from pdf.

Study Plan Creation

SISTEM offers a unique feature that enables students to easily create their study plans by submitting their grades. The system automatically generates a personalized study plan, eliminating the need for students to manually add courses. The "Actions" column indicates whether the student has passed a course, is ineligible to take a course, or is eligible to enroll in a course.

Access

Students can access the website if they are enrolled in USC, and a member of DCISM
(Department of Computer and Information Sciences and Mathematics)
